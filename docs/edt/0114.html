Moving by Lines

Typing [Up] or [Down] moves the input position back or forward one line at a time.

Moving back places the input position on the previous line. Moving forward places the input position on the next line.

To try these out, use [Up] and [Down] multiple times and observe how the input position changes.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
