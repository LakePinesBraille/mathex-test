Opening the Tutorial

— Important! — Be sure you understand how to open the tutorial before you close it!

Typing [Esc] closes the tutorial and returns to the editor, and typing [Alt+Shift+T] opens the tutorial where you left off.

To try these out, use [Esc] to close this page, then use [Alt+Shift+T] to come back.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
