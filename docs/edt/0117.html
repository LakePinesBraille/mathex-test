Moving to Document Start/End

Typing [Ctrl+Home] or [Ctrl+End] moves the input position up or down to the start or end of the document.

Moving up moves to the left of the first character in the document, and moving down moves to the right of the last character in the document.

To try these out, use [Ctrl+Home] and [Ctrl+End] multiple times to see how the input position changes.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
