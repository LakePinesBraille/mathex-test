Moving by Words

Typing [Ctrl+Right] or [Ctrl+Left] moves the input position forward or backward one word at a time.

Moving forward moves to the right of the last character of each word, and moving backward moves to the left of the first character of each word.

To try these out, use [Ctrl+Right] and [Ctrl+Left] multiple times to see how the input position changes.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
