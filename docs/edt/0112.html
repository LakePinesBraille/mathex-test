Moving by Characters

Typing [Right] or [Left] moves the input position forward or backward one character at a time.

Moving forward moves to the right of each character, and moving backward moves to the left of each character.

To try these out, use [Right] and [Left] multiple times to see how the input position changes.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
