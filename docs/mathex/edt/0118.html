Moving by Pages

Typing [PgUp] or [PgDn] moves the input position up or down one page at a time.

Moving up moves toward the top of the document, and moving down moves toward the end of the document.

These keys move the input position, and scroll the window to show more of the document.

To try these out, use [PgUp] and [PgDn] multiple times to see how the input position changes.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
