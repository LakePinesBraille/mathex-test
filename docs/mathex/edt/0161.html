Using the Panels

The editor panels organize menus, buttons, palettes, and other information. They may be shown or hidden as desired.

Typing [Alt+Shift+X] opens the panels if they are hidden, and closes the panels if they are showing.

To try this out, use [Alt+Shift+X] a few times to see the effect.

Press [Alt+Enter] when you are ready to continue.
