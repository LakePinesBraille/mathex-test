Getting To Know the Equalize Editor

[Help] Samples - EE Samples Folder

Open the EE Samples folder shared in Google Drive.

The EE Samples folder contains sample files that can be opened using the EE.

When you open the EE Samples folder the first time, it is added to your Google Drive under Shared with me. You can use Google Drive to organize the folder under My Drive as you would any other folder.

Once the EE Samples folder is added to your Google Drive, you can use the EE Drive Menu to open the sample files using the EE.

Type Alt Shift H, M to open the EE Samples folder.
